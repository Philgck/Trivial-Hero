Trivial Hero

Instructions
Welcome to Trivial Hero!

In this game, you will face off against a villain in a battle of wits. Answer trivia questions correctly to attack the villain and reduce their health. Be careful, though! If you answer incorrectly, the villain will attack you.

How to Play:
  • Select a category and difficulty level from the settings menu.
  • Enter your hero's name.
  • Click "Start Game" to begin.
  • Answer the trivia questions by clicking on one of the four answer buttons.
  • If you answer correctly, your hero will attack the villain. If you answer incorrectly, the villain will attack your hero.
  • The game ends when either the hero or the villain's health reaches zero.
Tips:
  • Pay attention to the question category and difficulty level to strategize your answers.
  • Use the "Start Again" button to reset the game and try different strategies.

Good luck, and may the best trivia master win!
